• NAVIGATION:
  • Mouse click – Move view to clicked point
  • Mouse scroll – Move in viewing direction
  • Middle mouse button – Hold down to maintain constant velocity
  • Arrow keys – Camera rotation in local axes
  • Ctrl + Arrows – Faster camera rotation (5x)
  • +/- – Zoom in/out (changes FOV)
  • Space – Immediate stop / Pause
  • A – Enable/disable camera animations
  • FRACTAL PARAMETERS:
  • R – Reset fractal parameters (random c)
  • 0 – Start/Stop Slice parameter animation (4D)
  • </> – Decrease/Increase Slice animation range (amplitude)
  • 9 – Cycle through cross-section modes
  • [/] – Decrease/Increase cross-section distance
  • RENDERING QUALITY:
  • 1/2 – Increase/Decrease iteration count (±20)
  • 3 – Soft shadows (on/off)
  • 4 – Ambient Occlusion (on/off)
  • 5 – Smooth coloring (on/off)
  • 6 – Cycle color palettes (OFF + 10 different color schemes)
  • 7 – Specular highlight (on/off)
  • 8 – Adaptive Ray Marching (on/off)
  • INTERFACE & RECORDING:
  • M – Show/Hide this menu
  • P – Show/Hide stats panel
  • G – Show/Hide advanced controls panel
  • S – Take a screenshot of the fractal
  • V – Start/Stop video recording
  • Q – Change recording quality (normal/high/ultra)
  • T – Open tour recording menu
  • Esc – Stop tour playback
  • ADVANCED COLOR EFFECTS:
  • SHIFT + C – Toggle color animation
  • SHIFT + S – Color saturation (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + B – Color brightness (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + N – Color contrast (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + P – Color phase shift (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + A – Color animation speed (+CTRL to decrease)
  • ORBIT TRAP EFFECTS:
  • SHIFT + O – Toggle orbit trap effects
  • SHIFT + T – Cycle orbit trap types
  • SHIFT + R – Orbit trap radius (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + I – Orbit trap intensity (+CTRL to decrease)
  • PHYSICS-BASED COLORING:
  • SHIFT + F – Toggle physics-based coloring
  • SHIFT + Y – Cycle physics color types
  • SHIFT + Q – Physics frequency (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + W – Physics waves (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + E – Physics intensity (+CTRL to decrease)
  • SHIFT + D – Physics balance (+CTRL to decrease)
  • QUICK ACCESS:
  • O – Toggle orbit trap (quick access)
  • F – Toggle physics color (quick access)
  • C – Toggle color animation (quick access)
